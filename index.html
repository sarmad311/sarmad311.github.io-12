About Me

I am currently Head of the Center for Policy and Area Studies at Institute of Business Management (IoBM). I hold a PhD and MS in Economics from Institute of Business Management. I earned my MA in English Literature from the University of Karachi. I have taught at several reputable educational institutes in Pakistan. I have also written articles for newspapers. I has presented papers at national and international conferences. My paper “Incoherences in Konrad Lorenz’s Concept of Aggression” has been cited in Jai Galliott’s Commercial Space Exploration: Ethics Policy and Governance. My paper “Iqbal’s Ideal Critique of Hawking’s Materialist Concept of Time” was published in Iqbal Review, April 2008 issue. My works have also been published in Pakistan Business Review, Business Review, and Journal of Education and Educational Development, Journal of Islamic Thought and Civilization. I received the best paper award (including all categories) in the area of General Management in the International Research Conference on Contemporary Management Practices 2013 held by Lahore University of Management Sciences (LUMS). I compose poetry in Urdu and have published a collection of my poetic works by the title, Saboocha. This collection includes Urdu translations of some of the world-renowned poets such as W.B. Yeats, T.S. Eliot and Robert Frost. My research works address contemporary economic, political, and moral issues with reference to pre-modern, modern and postmodern philosophy.

Award & Distinction
